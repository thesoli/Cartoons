Monsters, Inc. main characters

This is a list of characters from the Disney/Pixar media franchise Monsters, Inc.

James Patrick Sullivan

James P. Sullivan, also known as Jimmy or Sulley, is one of the main characters of Monsters, Inc. and its prequel, Monsters University. He also returned as a main character in the series Monsters At Work. He is a large mammalian monster with shaggy aqua fur with purple blotches, two small horns on his head, and a long tail.

Mary Gibbs

Mary Gibbs, better known as Boo, is the tritagonist of the 2001 Disney•Pixar animated film, Monsters, Inc.. She is a young girl who is not scared of any monsters except occasionally Randall, who happened to be her monster. Boo eventually ventures through her closet to enter the monster world, where she develops a bond with Sulley and Mike Wazowski.

Mike Wazowski

Mike Wazowski is one of the main characters in Monsters, Inc. and its prequel, Monsters University. He also returned as a main character in the series, Monsters at Work. He is a short, lime green spherical monster with one large green eye, two small horns on his head, and thin arms and legs attached to either side of his torso.
